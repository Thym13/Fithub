USE CASES


Progress Tracking & Goals
Gym Customers can monitor their fitness progress through FitHub. They can track weight loss, muscle gain, or other fitness goals. The system provides a visual representation of progress, helping customers stay motivated and on track to reach their objectives.

Payment & Billing Management
The system handles automatic billing for gym memberships and class fees. Members are notified when their subscription is about to expire, and the Gym Secretary can assist with payment processing. Gym Owners can access billing reports and track the gym’s financial performance through the platform’s analytics.

Class Scheduling
The Gym Secretary manages the class schedule, ensuring that all classes are properly organized, and that trainers are assigned to specific sessions. Members can view class schedules and book their spots through the FitHub portal.

Shop & Merchandise Sales
Gym Customers can purchase products such as gym apparel, supplements, or accessories from the gym’s online store. The Gym Secretary or Shop Owner manages inventory and ensures that items are in stock, while FitHub tracks sales transactions and stock levels.

Employee Task Assignment
Administrators assign tasks to staff members, such as greeting members at the entrance, ensuring cleanliness in the gym, or managing equipment. FitHub helps track task completion, ensuring that staff are performing their duties efficiently.

Lead Conversion & CRM
The Gym Secretary tracks potential leads and converts them into paying members. FitHub helps manage communication with leads, sending promotional offers and reminders to encourage them to sign up for memberships or book classes.

Member Feedback & Ratings
After attending classes or personal training sessions, Gym Customers can leave feedback or rate the services provided by Trainers and the gym overall. The Gym Owner and Administrator use this feedback to improve the gym’s offerings and customer service.
